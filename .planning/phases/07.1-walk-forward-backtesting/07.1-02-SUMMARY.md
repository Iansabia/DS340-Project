### Key Findings

1. **Naive baselines go negative with fees** -- the strongest sanity check. Previously reported as Sharpe ~12-13, now correctly shown as losing strategies (-2.25).
2. **Tier 1/2 models cluster at Sharpe 8.3-8.7** -- Linear Regression, XGBoost, GRU, LSTM all perform similarly after proper capital normalization and fee deduction.
3. **XGBoost has lowest max drawdown (2.8%)** among profitable models -- best risk-adjusted performance.
4. **PPO-Raw Sharpe drops from 14.0 to 6.0** but remains profitable -- RL shows some value but less than regression.
5. **PPO-Filtered remains worst trained model** -- anomaly filtering hurts rather than helps at 5% flagging rate.
6. **Win rates are 35-46%** -- models profit from magnitude of wins exceeding losses, not frequency.
7. **Break-even fees 12-20pp** for all models -- significant margin above 5pp actual costs.

## Decisions Made

- Single seed=42 used for all Tier 2/3 models to produce one clean equity curve per model (3-seed aggregation deferred to detailed analysis)

The high Sharpe reflects the quality-filtered academic dataset and the fact that ~20 trades/day at $100 each provides high exposure relative to $10K capital.
