## Decisions Made
- Lazy model training: models trained on first run_cycle() call, not in constructor -- allows fast instantiation and separate train/infer timing
- Tier 1 models predict on latest-bar-only flat features; Tier 2/3 models get full bar history through prepare_xy_for_seq for sequence context
- All predictions logged (trade=true when |pred|>threshold, trade=false otherwise) to track total signal volume
- Win rate and P&L show N/A when resolution data unavailable (single bar per pair has no next-bar to compute actual P&L against)
